The EthStaker knowledge base has [a good overview of hardware requirements](https://docs.ethstaker.org/hardware/hardware-requirements) summarized below, but please follow the link for more in-depth information.

#### Hardware requirements

Component  | Minimum Requirements
-----------| --------------------
OS         | Linux is recommended
RAM        | Minimum 16 GB
Hard drive | 2 TB SSD (no HDDs!)
CPU        | Minimum 6000 score on Passmark
USB        | Minimum 2 GB
